# Beam — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Who we are
Beam is a festival friend-finder app developed by Emil Kornerup, based in Denmark. Contact: emilkornerup@gmail.com

## What data we collect
- Your email address
- Your username
- Your GPS location

## Why we collect it
Your email and username identify your account. Your location is used to show your friends where you are so they can find you at festivals.

## Who can see your data
Only friends you have personally approved in the app can see your location. You can turn off location sharing per friend at any time.

## Where data is stored
All data is stored on Firebase (Google) servers in Belgium (europe-west1), within the European Union, in compliance with GDPR.

## Your rights under GDPR
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. You can delete your account and all data directly in the app under Settings → Delete My Account.

## Data retention
Your data is stored as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all data is permanently removed.

## Third parties
We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and push notifications. We do not sell your data to any third parties.
